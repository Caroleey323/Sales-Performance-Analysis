# Sales-Performance-Analysis
## Business Context
A retail company wanted to understand how its products were performing across different regions in order to improve sales and inventory planning.

## Dataset
The dataset contains sales transactions with the following fields:
Date
Product
Category
Region
Units Sold
Revenue

## Tasks Performed
- Organised and cleaned raw sales data
- Calculated total revenue and total units sold
- Used SUMIF to calculate revenue by product and region
- Identified the best-selling product and best-performing region
- Created visual charts to support the analysis

## Key Findings
Total revenue for the period was R1,127,000. Phone was the best-selling product, generating the highest revenue among all products. South Africa was the best performing region, contributing the largest share of sales.

## Recommendation
It is recommended that the company focuses inventory and marketing efforts on high-performing products and regions in order to maximise revenue.
